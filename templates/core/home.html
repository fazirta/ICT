Frequently Asked Questions

ICT stands for "Information Communication and Technology". An extracurricular in MAN 4 Jakarta that unfolds and evolve students interest in Technology.

ICT extracurricular activities differ from others by focusing on technology, practical skills development, and fostering creativity and innovation.

By completing the registration process, you gain access to a vibrant community of technology enthusiasts, where you can develop your skills, explore new horizons, and unleash your potential in the exciting realm of ICT.

Passion for technology is the primary criterion for joining an ICT extracurricular.
